# Month-End Savings Transfer

The Month-End Savings Transfer feature helps users build savings by automatically moving unused budget balances into a savings envelope at the end of each month.

This encourages disciplined budgeting while rewarding users who stay within their planned spending limits.

---

## Feature Concept

Users allocate monthly budgets across multiple spending envelopes such as:

- Food
- Travel
- Shopping
- Entertainment

If any envelope has remaining balance at the end of the month, the unused amount is automatically transferred into the user's savings envelope.

---

## Example Scenario

Monthly Budget Allocation:

Food → ₹8,000  
Travel → ₹3,000  
Shopping → ₹4,000  

End of Month Spending:

Food spent → ₹7,200  
Remaining → ₹800  

Travel spent → ₹2,500  
Remaining → ₹500  

Shopping spent → ₹3,600  
Remaining → ₹400  

Total savings transferred:

₹800 + ₹500 + ₹400 = ₹1,700

This amount is automatically moved into the savings envelope.

---

## Benefits

- Encourages responsible spending
- Rewards users for staying within budget
- Helps build savings automatically
- Reinforces positive financial habits

---

## Implementation Flow

End of Month Trigger  
↓  
System evaluates remaining balances in all envelopes  
↓  
Remaining balances are calculated  
↓  
Total remaining amount transferred to savings envelope  
↓  
New month budgets reset
