# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
Smart Budget Wallet

## Overview

Smart Budget Wallet is a digital envelope budgeting system designed for modern payment applications. 

The feature allows users to allocate their money into predefined spending categories such as food, travel, shopping, and savings. Each transaction automatically deducts from the corresponding category, helping users track and control their spending.

---

## Problem Statement

Digital payment apps make transactions extremely convenient, but they often do not help users manage their spending effectively.

When all funds exist as a single digital balance, users may lose track of category-based spending.

Common problems include:

- Overspending on food delivery or shopping
- Lack of visibility into monthly spending categories
- Difficulty maintaining savings goals

---

## Target Users

1. Young professionals managing monthly salaries
2. Students managing allowances
3. Families planning monthly household budgets

---

## Key Features

### 1. Digital Envelope Budgeting

Users can create spending envelopes such as:

- Rent
- Food
- Travel
- Shopping
- Entertainment
- Savings

Each envelope has a monthly allocation.

---

### 2. Automatic Transaction Categorization

Transactions are categorized automatically based on merchant.

Example:

Swiggy → Food  
Uber → Travel  
Amazon → Shopping

---

### 3. Budget Tracking

Users can view:

- Remaining budget in each category
- Monthly spending breakdown
- Budget usage percentage

---

### 4. Budget Alerts

The system sends notifications when:

- 80% of a category budget is used
- Budget is fully exhausted

---

### 5. Monthly Reset

At the start of each month:

- Budgets reset to allocated values
- Remaining balances may optionally move to savings

---

## Success Metrics

The success of this feature could be measured by:

- Percentage of users creating budgets
- Reduction in overspending behavior
- Increase in user savings rate
